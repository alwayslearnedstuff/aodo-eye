About AODO

AODO
The Association of Ophthalmology Development Officers is a network of development professionals affiliated with departments of ophthalmology and eye institutes at leading universities. Focused solely on the challenges and opportunities associated with academic ophthalmology development, AODO Members network informally throughout the year and hold an annual meeting during the AAO (American Academy of Ophthalmology) national meeting to exchange fundraising information, marketing strategies, professional knowledge, experience, and to collaborate on group-identified issues.
